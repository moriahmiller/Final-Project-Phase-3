What we are all about!

The Coffee Bean and Tea Leaf is an American coffee chain founded in 1963. It is owned and operated by International Coffee and Tea, LLC, which has its corporate headquarters in Los Angeles, California.

  • Our Coffee and Tea
  • Coffee: Colombia Narino, Costa Ricka, Italian Espressp, Pumpkin Spice, Good Morning Blend
  • Tea: Green, Black, Oolong, Herbal Infusion, Tropical Passion, Yin, Orchard Peach
