History

Born and brewed in Southern California since 1963, The Coffee Bean and Tea Leaf is the oldest and largest privately held specialty coffee and tea retailer in the United States.

Though we travel far and wide to discover the flavors you love, we always bring it home. Delivering the look, taste and aroma of the world's best coffees and teas is what we do best, and every sip should take you to the corners of the earth where each flavor is cultivated. Let us pour you something delicious.
